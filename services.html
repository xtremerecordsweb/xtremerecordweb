our services

From recording and mixing to training and event coverage — X-Treme Music Studio offers a full suite of professional services built for artists, creators, and brands.

01
Music Recording
Professional recording sessions for artists, bands, and vocalists. Our studio is equipped with industry-standard gear to capture your sound at the highest quality.
02
Movie Soundtrack / Voiceover
Original soundtrack composition and professional voiceover recording for film, television, and digital content productions.
03
Mixing & Mastering
Expert mixing and mastering services to give your music the polished, radio-ready sound it deserves. We balance every element for the best listening experience across all platforms.
04
Radio Jingles & Commercial
Catchy, high-impact jingles and commercial audio produced for radio stations, brands, and advertising campaigns.
05
Instrumental Recording
Live and studio instrumental recording for all genres. From solo instruments to full arrangements, we bring your musical vision to life.
06
Music Production Training
Hands-on training for aspiring producers and beatmakers. Learn music production, sound design, and studio workflow from experienced professionals.
07
Event Coverage
Live audio recording and production coverage for concerts, shows, corporate events, and special occasions.
08
Karaoke Live Session
Hosted karaoke live sessions for events, entertainment nights, and private bookings — powered by professional sound and atmosphere.
09
Video Editing & More
Professional video editing services for music videos, promotional content, and creative projects. Plus additional creative services tailored to your needs.
