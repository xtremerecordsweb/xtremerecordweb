Artist
Xalor
Afro-Fusion · Street

Xalor, born Gideon Ugonna Henry, is a Nigerian artist signed to Xtreme Recordz, emerging from Aba South, Abia State, carving out a distinct lane within the evolving African music scene.

Born on 22 March 2005 in Aba, Nigeria, his sound is rooted in Afro-fusion, infused with street-inspired rhythms that reflect both the realities and aspirations of his generation.

Raised in the culturally rich and industrious city of Aba, Xalor draws strong influence from his environment, translating its energy, hustle, and resilience into his music.

His style blends infectious melodies with introspective lyricism, creating records that are both engaging and emotionally grounded.

From an early age, he developed a deep passion for music, using it as a tool for expression and escape, and over time, he has refined a sound that is both relatable and distinctive.

Whether delivering smooth, melodic records or high-energy street anthems, Xalor connects naturally with listeners through authenticity and storytelling.

With a growing presence and a clear artistic direction, he continues to build his brand while expanding his reach beyond local boundaries.

As he evolves, Xalor stands as a promising voice of a new generation, representing Aba with ambition, creativity, and global intent.

Artist
Chriz Kingx
Afrobeats · Trap · Hip-Hop · Dancehall

Paul Christian Chibuifeniile, professionally known as CHRIZ KINGX, is a Nigerian artist, singer, and songwriter, signed to Xtreme Recordz.

Born on 11 September 2005 in Lagos, Nigeria, and originally from Anambra State, CHRIZ KINGX draws influence from both the vibrant energy of Lagos and the cultural depth of his roots.

His music is shaped by real-life experiences, emotion, and ambition, reflecting a journey of growth, resilience, and self-expression.

Blending Afrobeats with elements of trap, hip hop, dancehall, reggae, and pop, he delivers a versatile and boundary-pushing sound that sets him apart within the new wave of Nigerian artists.

With a bold vocal presence and introspective lyricism, CHRIZ KINGX has developed a distinct artistic identity rooted in authenticity, storytelling, and emotional depth.

Recognised for his strong songwriting ability, his music balances raw honesty with confident delivery, creating records that resonate both emotionally and sonically.

His work is defined by melodic richness, relatable narratives, and a fearless approach to translating life experiences into music.

As he prepares to release his debut single, CHRIZ KINGX stands as one of the promising voices of Nigeria's new generation, driven by purpose, passion, and a clear artistic vision.
